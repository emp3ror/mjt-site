description: "Charting movement lines before committing to contour."
date: "2024-05-20"
updated: "2024-05-28"
---

1. Start with three 30-second gestures focusing only on line of action.
2. Overlay a second pass that catches weight distribution and balance.
3. Annotate with arrows describing force and counter-force.

> The goal is rhythm, not accuracy. Accuracy arrives once rhythm is second nature.
